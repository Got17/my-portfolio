## Question
Which design aesthetic (Sleek Dark Mode or Minimalist & Clean) is preferred by the user for their portfolio? 

To resolve this, we will build a side-by-side or toggleable static HTML/CSS/JS prototype containing a sample Hero and Project card in both styles, allowing the user to view and choose their favorite.

## Deliverables
- A prototype folder containing sample sections for both styles.
- A user decision on the final portfolio aesthetic.
